# Analyzing Webb Composition

This skill is for decomposition and diagnosis, not for color grading.

## Focus Areas

- foreground, midground, and background strength
- subject separation
- color and light tension
- edge pressure
- narrative density
- Alex Webb fit

## Required References

- `../../style-packs/composition/alex-webb/knowledge/layering-foundations.md`
- `../../style-packs/composition/alex-webb/knowledge/cropping-guardrails.md`

## Rules

- Explain why the frame does or does not support Webb-like cropping.
- Do not claim that color alone makes the frame Webb-like.
- If the frame is fundamentally weak for Webb, say so directly.
