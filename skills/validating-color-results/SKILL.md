# Validating Color Results

Review the draft before returning it.

## Checklist

- The user-selected Fujifilm style is explicit.
- Guided image flow did not skip the menu gate.
- Official Fujifilm semantics were treated as the definition layer.
- Base simulation effects are separated from supporting settings.
- Compatibility is conditional where necessary.
- Previews and exports are labeled approximate.
- `next_test_to_run` is concrete and reproducible.
- The response matches `../../skills/shared-assets/result-contract.md`.
