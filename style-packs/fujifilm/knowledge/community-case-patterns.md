# Community Case Patterns

FujiDay can learn from community recipe patterns without treating them as official truth.

Repeated patterns worth tracking:

- Classic Chrome often pairs with restrained color and slight warm/red shifts for editorial documentary looks.
- ASTIA often stays soft in highlight handling for portrait-safe rendering.
- ETERNA often stays muted and low-contrast for cinematic stills.
- Classic Neg. often uses denser shadows and cooler shifts for nostalgic everyday mood.

Compatibility notes should stay conditional because controls and base simulations vary by Fujifilm body generation.
