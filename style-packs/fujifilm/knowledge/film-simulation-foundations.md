# Film Simulation Foundations

FujiDay v1 centers on seven Fujifilm style families:

- `PROVIA / Standard`
  Balanced, neutral, broad-use baseline.
- `Velvia / Vivid`
  Saturated, scenic, high-impact color.
- `ASTIA / Soft`
  Softer contrast and friendlier portrait rendering.
- `Classic Chrome`
  Restrained color and editorial documentary character.
- `Classic Neg.`
  Nostalgic negative-film feel with denser shadows.
- `ETERNA`
  Muted, low-contrast, cinematic rendering.
- `ACROS`
  Strong monochrome depth with texture emphasis.

When FujiDay reasons about a look, it separates:

1. what the base Film Simulation is doing
2. what the supporting settings are doing

That split is mandatory. A recipe is not treated as a magical preset.
